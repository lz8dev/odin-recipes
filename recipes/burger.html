LZ's Burger

The LZ's perfect & simple burger

Fast to make and low cost ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Onion

Steps:

  • Open bread
  • Cook meat
  • Put lettuce on lower bread
  • Put the meat above
  • Put the cheese
  • Next put tomato
  • Now more lettuce
  • Put a couple onion rings
  • Finish with the upper bread
