LZ Hot Dog

The LZ's perfect & simple Hot Dog

Fast to make and low cost ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Sausage
  • Ripped potato chips
  • Cheese
  • Mustard or others

Steps:

  • Cook the sausage
  • Put the sausage into the bread
  • Put the cheese
  • Next put the potato
  • Now the cheese
  • Finish with the sauce
